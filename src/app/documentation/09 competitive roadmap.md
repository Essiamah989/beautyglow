# 09 - Competitive Roadmap & Future Enhancements

This document outlines strategic enhancements designed to create a "moat" for BeautyGlow, making it difficult for competitors to match our value proposition in the Tunisian and international markets.

## 1. AI-Powered Automation (Pro/Elite)
Move from a "tool" to an "automated business partner."

- **AI Social Copilot**: In the Service/Photo management section, add a "Generate for Instagram" button. Use AI to craft engaging post captions based on service details and photos.
- **AI Smart Scheduling**: Detect "dead zones" in the calendar and automatically suggest sending a limited-time promo (via email/WhatsApp) to "Inactive" clients.
- **AI Review Responder**: Automatically draft professional responses to customer testimonials.

## 2. Local Market Specialization (Tunisian Moat)
Generic international tools (Treatwell, Fresha) often miss local cultural and technological nuances.

- **WhatsApp Integration**: 
    - One-click booking confirmations.
    - Automated appointment reminders 2 hours before.
    - "Book via WhatsApp" floating button on public sites.
- **Local Payment Tracking**: Advanced dashboard for tracking cash/cheque payments since digital payment adoption varies.
- **Language Localization**: Full Arabic interface for the dashboard to empower non-French speaking salon owners.

## 3. Customer Retention & Loyalty
Lock customers into the platform by making their history valuable.

- **Digital VIP Engine**: Automatic loyalty point tracking (e.g., 10% of price = points).
- **Referral Loops**: "Give 10 TND, Get 10 TND" personal links for clients to invite friends.
- **Push Notifications**: If a mobile app shell is added later, use push for "Last minute cancellations" to fill slots.

## 4. Multi-Tenant / Franchise Management (Elite Only)
Support larger businesses with multiple branches.

- **Centralized Dashboard**: See analytics for all 5 salon locations in one view.
- **Staff Floating**: Allow staff members to be assigned to different locations on different days.
- **Global Inventory**: Share product inventory (shampoos, dyes) across branches.
